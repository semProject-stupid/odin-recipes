Frog Soup Recipe

Description:

This recipe is for a delicious frog soup. After a long day of casting curses on people, this is just the perfect pick-me-up! Quiet easy and quick to make too. Let's get started!

Ingredients:

  • Frogs - 5
  • Pond Water - 2 cups
  • Lotus Flower - 1 (medium)
  • Salt - 1 tsp

Steps:

  1. Pour the pond water in a large pot.
  2. Add finely crushed lotus flowers, followed by salt. You can add more flowers or salt as per your taste.
  3. Stir until water starts to boil.
  4. Once boiled, take it off the stove and let it cool.
  5. Done!

You can now serve it to the frogs you invited. Did you really think we were going to boil them alive. Man. Sorry I should have given it a less horrifying name.
