Very Berry Strawberry Shake

Description:

This recipe is best for slithery, slimey creatures. Jumpscaring and terrorizing people takes a lot of energy. So to get that energy back, please enjoy this refreshing milkshake!

Ingredients:

  • 5 Strawberries - (not washed for extra protein from bugs)
  • 5 Blueberries - (same as above)
  • 5 Raspberries - (same as above)
  • 1 cup milk - (chilled)

Steps:

  1. Chew the strawberries.
  2. Chew the blueberries.
  3. Chew the raspberries.
  4. Drink milk on top of all the berries.

Please remember that slimey creatures don't have fingers. Nor blenders. Happy biting!
