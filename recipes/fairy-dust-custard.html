Fairy Dust Custard

Description:

Sometimes I'm craving something sweet but I don't know what to make. So here is a quick recipe for those times (everyday).

Ingredients:

  • Fairy Dust - 1 pound
  • An empty bowl

Steps:

  1. Keep the bowl in front of you.
  2. Pour some fairy dust in the bowl.
  3. Close your eyes and picture the dessert in your mind.
  4. Open your eyes!

Pretty easy to make right? DISCLAIMER: This recipe was only reported to work for individuals who weren't taking their medication.
